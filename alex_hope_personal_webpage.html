Biography

Dr. Alexander Hope is an Associate Professor of Business Ethics and Head of the Department of Leadership and HRM at Northumbria University. With a background in Environmental Management and Sustainable Development, Dr. Hope has made significant contributions to the fields of business ethics, responsible management education, and sustainable development. He is actively involved in various international initiatives, including the United Nations PRME and has a proven track record of leadership and change management in academic and professional settings.

Research Interests

Dr. Hope's research focuses on responsible business, sustainable development, corporate social responsibility (CSR), responsible management education, sustainable project management, and energy policy. He is currently engaged in projects related to international climate change education and the effectiveness of net-positive business and conscious capitalism movements.

Dr. Hope's teaching interests include responsible business, responsible management education, sustainable development, corporate social responsibility (CSR), responsible strategy, environmental management, and sustainable project management. He leads various modules such as Applied Business Ethics, Corporate Social Responsibility for Multi-Nationals, and Sustainable Project Management.
